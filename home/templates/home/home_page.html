Why “Bitesize”?

Church and Christianity can sometimes feel overwhelming or intimidating—especially if you’re new or just curious. At Bitesize Church, we believe faith should be easy to explore, welcoming for everyone, and full of joy.

That’s why we keep things simple, friendly, and relaxed. And yes, we share a free brunch together every time we meet—because good food brings people together and helps us connect.

Whether you’re a lifelong Christian, just starting to ask questions, or simply hungry for community (and brunch!), you’re welcome here.
